# Example audits — gold-standard references

Two real RankPrompt audits, kept as the reference for what a great deliverable looks like. The `citation-audit` skill and `knowledge/brain.md` point here so the copilot matches this depth and structure.

| Folder | What it shows | The mechanism it found |
|---|---|---|
| `quiet-events-city-audit/` | A city-by-city citation audit across 10 markets — visibility + citation share per segment, source/page-type mix, per-city cited URLs, the page-vs-page autopsy, and the rebuild plan. | **Page-quality / intent** — the city pages existed but were thin, near-duplicate, and framed for "silent disco" while buyers search "silent conference," so AI discounted them and cited competitors. |
| `owings-auto-location-audit/` | A local (two-metro) AI-visibility audit — where the brand ranks in each local field, top cited domains + content types, own cited pages, and why it wins one market but not the other. | **Footprint** — the on-page content was fine; the lagging market simply had too few third-party profiles citing the brand. |

Each folder has the source `report.html` and the rendered PDF. Read them to calibrate a new `citation-audit` before writing one.
