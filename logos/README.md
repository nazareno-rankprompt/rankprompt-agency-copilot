# Logos

RankPrompt's own logos ship here (used as the default branding on deliverables).

## White-label with your agency logo

To brand every report (matrix board, citation audit, task board) as your agency:

- Run **`/agency-brand youragency.com`** — it sets up your name + brand color and fetches your logo when it can, or
- Just **drop your logo here** as **`agency-logo.png`** (a transparent PNG or SVG wordmark works best).

Your branding **works with just your name + color even without a logo** — the reports show your agency name and re-theme to your brand color. The logo is an enhancement, so you're never blocked if it can't be fetched automatically (some environments, like Claude Desktop's sandbox, block image downloads — just drop the file here or attach it in chat).

`agency-logo.png` and your `branding.json` are gitignored — they're yours and never get pushed to the template.
